About us

KUJA is the first e-commerce platform that allows all Rwandans to join the world of online purchase without the need of a credit card. By simply using your mobile money you will be allowed to shop internationally finding the cheapest options for your purchases of goods, flight tickets, Hotel reservation, Drugs etc .

Vision
To grant to all Rwandans people to access the world of e-commerce without the need of a credit card or bank transaction.
Our history

KUJA is a start-up created in 2019 by passionate young entreprenuers , aiming at expanding the access to the new technologies to all peole in Rwanda and elsewhere.

  • With increasing importance of online stores,We facilitates individuals with no credit cards to be able to purchase online.
  • Every delivery need a depository, yet very few have post office account
  • Our clients can interract with us physically, which increases trust.
  • We partner with trusted agencies such as post office to deliver goods.
